Beer Terms

Hops: A vine-like plant. Humulus Lupulus

IBU: International Bittering Units. The measurement scale for hops.

Domestic: The “Big dog” American brewing companies.

Pilsner: A type of lager

Craft: A small independent or traditional brewery.

Import: Anything not made in the USA

Wort: The liquid extracted from the mashing process that contains maltose and maltotriose sugars; which are fermented into alcohol by the yeast.

Malt: Grain that has been specially prepared for brewing.
